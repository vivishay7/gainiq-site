How It Works

GainIQ connects directly with tools like Jira, Slack, and Salesforce to detect where AI is saving time. Those freed hours are then intelligently routed to your highest-priority projects using our dynamic Gantt engine. Once results hit, our platform calculates the dollar-value impact—proving your AI’s ROI in hard numbers.

The Product

From onboarding to ROI reporting, GainIQ was built to be frictionless for ops teams, managers, and finance. The platform includes:

  • Capacity Dashboard with team/time insights
  • Gantt overlay for smart redeployments
  • Tiered ROI proof system (A/B/C evidence standard)
  • Audit-ready reports with finance-grade transparency

Proof Your CFO Can Trust

Each redeployment comes with a full paper trail: freed time → approval → work shipped → KPI moved → dollar value realized. Our confidence-tier system keeps it real, auditable, and frictionless to present at any board meeting.
